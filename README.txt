A 'music' search form plugin, placed in your wordpress sidebar, that channels your favorite musical artist's tracks as well as their music videos.  


== Description ==

Search your favorite music artists through a search form widget located on your wordpress sidebar. Each music search provides a  feed of the top 50 tracks of your favorite artists. you can also search music feeds by 'top artists' or by music category(genre)
-- Hip Hop, Rap, Disco, Blues, Country and much more. Each artist track clicks-through to a feed of youtube music videos.

 
= Check out the working example at Mixleymusic =  

* type your favorite music artist in the form at [mixley music](http://www.mixleymusic.com/?page_id=2 "mixleymusic widget") sidebar.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Click 'Plugins => Add New'. 
2. Search for 'Mixley Music & Video (Music Selection Form)'.
3. Click 'Install' button. 
4. Activate 'Mixley Music & Video' from your Plugins page.
 

= From WordPress.org =
1. Download 'Mixley Music & Video' directory.
2. Upload the 'Mixley Music & Video'(mixley directory) to your   '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...).
3. Activate Mixley Music & Video' from your Plugins page.
